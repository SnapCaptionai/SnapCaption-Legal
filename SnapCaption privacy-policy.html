Privacy Policy — AI Creator Studio

📅 Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: Shatica R Miles

SnapCaption ("the App") is an AI-powered content creation tool built for creators. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

SnapCaption is designed with privacy first. Here is what the app collects:

  • Usage data stored locally on your device only: Number of content generations, daily streaks, saved content, and app preferences. This data never leaves your device.
  • Content you enter: Topics, captions, scripts, and other content you type into the app are sent to an AI API (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or Google Gemini depending on your settings) to generate results. We do not store or log this content on our servers.
  • API keys (optional): If you add your own API keys in Settings, they are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to us.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identification
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes
  • We do not store your generated content on any server

3. Third-Party AI Services

When you generate content using the app, your prompts are sent to third-party AI providers. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • Anthropic (Claude): anthropic.com/privacy
  • OpenAI (ChatGPT): openai.com/privacy
  • Google (Gemini): policies.google.com/privacy

4. Advertising

The free version of SnapCaption may display ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. Pro users are completely ad-free.

For more information on Google AdMob's data practices, visit: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Local Data Storage

SnapCaption stores the following data locally on your device using browser/app local storage:

  • Number of content generations and daily streak count
  • Content you choose to save using the ⭐ Save feature
  • Your app preferences (selected AI engine, theme preferences)
  • Daily usage count for the free tier limit

This data is stored only on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

SnapCaption is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Since SnapCaption does not collect personal data on our servers, there is no account data to access or export. All your locally stored data can be cleared at any time by going to Settings → Clear All Saved Data within the app, or by uninstalling the app.

To request deletion of any data associated with your use of the app, please contact us using the information below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
