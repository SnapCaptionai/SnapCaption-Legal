🔒 Your Data & Your Rights

SnapCaption – AI Creator Studio is developed by Shatica R Miles. We take your privacy seriously. This page explains how to delete your data and what happens when you do.

SnapCaption stores all user data locally on your device only. We do not maintain accounts or personal profiles on any server. This means most of your data can be deleted instantly, directly from the app.

How to Delete Your Data

1

Delete directly in the app (Instant)

Open SnapCaption → tap Settings ⚙️ → scroll to the bottom → tap "Clear All Saved Data". This immediately removes all locally stored data including saved content, streaks, usage history, and preferences.

2

Uninstall the app (Complete removal)

Uninstalling SnapCaption from your device removes all locally stored app data permanently. This is the most complete form of data deletion.

3

Submit a deletion request (Email)

If you believe any data associated with your use of SnapCaption exists beyond your device, you can submit a formal deletion request by emailing us. We will respond within 5 business days.
